The Best Movies According To Dewanshu Baijal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Rockstar (2011)

An Indian hindi movie with wonderful songs that have depth in them. Movie is about a struggling singer who is hustling to find his way but is not able to succeed. It has sense of spirituality and connection with the divine power. Movie also has depiction of senses and a lesson that everything is meant to happen when it is destined to be.

Do Lafzon ki Kahani

Again an Indian film starring one of the best actor Randeep Hooda is a romantic film about a boxer who used to do odd jobs to make his ends meet. He used to do life or other side type of street fights for money. This Movie has a sense of destiny when that guy unknowingly is responsible for making a girl blind, they both meet again after years without knowing their past and get fall in love with each other. Guy was working for money only because of this girl after knowing that he was responsible for her lost eyes as he wanted her eyes back which in the end he succeded but after a extremely tough journey through.

Joker (2019)

An iconic oscar winning film starring one of the acting greats Joaquin Phoenix. This movie depicts the harsh realities of life showing the person with the loudest laugh and a person who try .. try hard to bring smile on people's faces in public is the same person who used to cry deeply when alone. Truly a heartouching film. It's so easy to bully someone but we might not know what the person is going through and a little trigger negatively could put them in life of death situation turning on the wild in them as if 'either the person will stay or me'. A must watch for the people who take pride in insulting and who still have some part of the heart left.
